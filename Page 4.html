Before

First of all, congratulations, you are thinking in getting a new pet and you are in the right path for adoption. However, wanting a new furry friend it is only the first step, some things need to be arranged after. You can start with:

  • Making sure that you have enough space for your furry friend at home
  • Check with other household member for allergies or any discomfort related to animals
  • Consider the initials cost of the adoption process and pet supplies
  • Be prepared to invest time in making your new pet comfortable at home
  • Invest in good food and first aid medicines for your pet
